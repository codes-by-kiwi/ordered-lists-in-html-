3 ingredient peanut butter cookies

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup peanut butter
  2. 1 cup white sugar
  3. 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, egg until it is creamy.
  3. Roll it into small balls.
  4. Arrange on a baking sheet.
  5. Flatten it with a fork.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 10 mins.
  7. cool cookies on baking sheet for 2 mins.
  8. Move to a plate.
